Biography

Greetings! I am Wanru Zhao(赵婉如), a senior undergraduate student at Beihang University(Beijing, China), majoring in Computer Science and Engineering.

Currently, I’m focusing on the topic of self-supervised learning and visualization under the guidance of Prof. Chaoli Wang. Before that, I was working on the topic of crowdsourcing and big spatio-temporal data in dynamic pricing advised by Prof. Yongxin Tong . Besides, I also did a 6-month internship on compression and quantization of neural networks in Link and Compile Group(LCG) in SenseTime, Beijing.
